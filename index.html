My Journey

Embarking on my journey at Kodego, I delved into crafting the front-end elements using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap for our inaugural project. My role involved steering the development of the front-end codebase and bringing to life a fully-functional Resort Website from inception.

Transitioning to my second venture, I expanded my toolkit to include jQuery and JavaScript, taking on both front-end and back-end development for a specialized POS System tailored for ice cream establishments. Leading a collaborative effort, we seamlessly integrated frontend and backend architecture to deliver a comprehensive solution.

For my third endeavor, I harnessed a diverse set of tools, including Ajax, PHP, and MySQL, to spearhead the creation of an Inventory Management System (IMS) for a grocery and convenience store supplier. Taking the helm of front-end development, I orchestrated a team effort to build a robust system encompassing both frontend and backend components.
